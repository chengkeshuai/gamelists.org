Monster Survivors

Battle endless waves of monsters in this thrilling survival action game!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your objective is to survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. Choose from multiple unique characters, each with their own special abilities and play styles.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies. Collect experience orbs from defeated monsters to level up and choose powerful upgrades. Survive as long as possible and aim for the highest score!

Game Features

  • Multiple playable characters with unique abilities
  • Procedurally generated enemy waves
  • RPG-like progression system with upgrades
  • Challenging boss fights
  • Intuitive controls for both PC and mobile
